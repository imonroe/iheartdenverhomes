The Process

How I sell your home
the right way

From initial consultation to closed deal — a clear, proven process.

01

Pricing Strategy

Pricing is everything. I'll analyze recent comparable sales, current market inventory, and your home's unique attributes to arrive at a price that attracts buyers and maximizes your return.

02

Prep & Staging

Before a single photo is taken, I'll walk through your home and give you actionable advice on what to fix, what to stage, and what buyers in this market care about most.

03

Professional Marketing

Your home deserves professional photography, compelling listing copy, targeted social media promotion, and placement in front of qualified buyers — not just MLS exposure.

04

Showings & Open Houses

I coordinate all showings, attend open houses personally, and gather buyer feedback so we can adjust strategy in real time if needed.

05

Offer Review & Negotiation

When offers arrive, I'll walk you through every term — not just price — and negotiate hard on your behalf to secure the best possible outcome.

06

Smooth Close

From contract to closing, I manage the timeline, coordinate with title, lenders, and inspectors, and make sure nothing falls through the cracks.
