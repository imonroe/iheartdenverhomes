How It Works

The process behind
every transformation.

01
Walk & Assess
We walk every room together — eyes fresh, clipboard ready. What's working? What's not? What do buyers in this neighborhood actually respond to? That's the conversation.
02
Edit & Stage
Less is almost always more. We clear the clutter, bring in the right pieces, and style every surface with intention. This isn't decorating — it's product positioning.
03
Professional Photos
We shoot with a professional photographer — no iPhone photos, no overhead overhead lighting. The listing photos are your first showing, and we treat them like it.
04
Strategic Launch
We go live at the right time, priced right, with marketing that gets eyes on it immediately. The staging sets it up — the launch strategy brings buyers in.
