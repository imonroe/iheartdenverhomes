How It Works

From listed to sold — the process.

Every home I list goes through a preparation process designed to maximize your return.

01
Walk & Assess
We walk through the home together and I identify what buyers will focus on — and what we can do to influence their reaction.
02
Prep & Stage
From decluttering and touch-up painting to full staging, we get the home looking its absolute best before a single photo is taken.
03
Professional Photos
Every listing gets professional photography. The first showing happens online — and your photos need to stop the scroll.
04
Strategic Launch
We time and price the listing to generate maximum activity in the first weekend — when offers are strongest and competition is fiercest.
