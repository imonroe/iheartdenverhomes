The Person Behind the Listings

Hey there,
I'm Emily!

Denver realtor. Great Dane mom. Former Seattle dot-com project manager. Colorado "nativish" — grew up in Arvada, spent years in Seattle, came back and never left.

"My goal is to help you have the most stress-free real estate experience while finding your perfect home."

I grew up in Arvada, Colorado — 15 minutes outside Denver. After high school I spent a year at CU Boulder before dreams of the coast took me to the Pacific Northwest. I spent years working as a Project Manager during Seattle's dot-com boom, logging countless hours solving problems and managing complex timelines. I also volunteered extensively at an all-ages music venue, which taught me more about creative problem-solving than any business school ever could.

I returned to Denver in 2001 to be closer to family — and honestly, because Colorado gets into your blood. The choice to go into real estate was a natural transition: it combines the project management skills I honed in tech with the same creativity I used to solve last-minute concert crises. Getting a client to the closing table is its own kind of live performance.

I currently reside in a 1954 ranch in Wheat Ridge with my Great Danes, Valentine and Goldie. I genuinely love this city and everything around it — the 300 days of sunshine, the mountains 45 minutes away, the microbrews, the rooftop decks, the food scene. I can't wait to show you around.
